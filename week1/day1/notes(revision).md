# What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used to manage customers, sales, and business operations.
# What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
It helps companies manage:
- Customer information
- Sales
- Support
- Communication
# Why Companies Use Salesforce
- Improves customer management
- Automates workflows
- Tracks sales process
- Increases productivity
- Provides cloud access
# Account, Contact, Opportunity
## Account
Represents a company or organization.
## Contact
Represents a person associated with the account.
## Opportunity
Represents a business deal or sales opportunity.
# Business Workflow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer
